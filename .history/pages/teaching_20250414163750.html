Dr. Wang's teaching activities at the University of South Florida.

Courses Taught

CIS 6930: Security & Privacy in Machine Learning

Graduate Course

Focuses on the intersection of machine learning and security/privacy, covering topics like adversarial attacks, differential privacy, and federated learning. (Created by Dr. Wang)

Semesters Taught: Fall 2024, Fall 2023

CIS 4219: Human Aspects of Cybersecurity

Undergraduate Course

Explores the human element in cybersecurity, including social engineering, usability of security tools, and security awareness training.

Semesters Taught: Spring 2025, Spring 2024
